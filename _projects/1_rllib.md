[RLlib](https://docs.ray.io/en/latest/rllib/index.html){:target="_blank"} is the industry-standard open-source library for reinforcement learning, built on top of [Ray](https://www.ray.io/){:target="_blank"}. As technical owner at [Anyscale](https://www.anyscale.com){:target="_blank"}, I lead stability, performance, and correctness across RLlib's distributed training and inference stack.

**Key contributions:**
- Diagnosing and eliminating high-impact failure modes (hangs, deadlocks, non-determinism, resource leaks) in large-scale RL workloads
- Benchmark-driven performance engineering and regression-prevention guardrails
- CI stress tests, determinism checks, and performance gates at scale

**Stack:** Python, Ray, PyTorch, Kubernetes, distributed systems
